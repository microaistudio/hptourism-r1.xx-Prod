Frequently Asked Questions

How long does registration take?

Typically 7-14 working days after document verification and inspection.

What documents are required?

Aadhaar card, property documents, photos of rooms, and NOC (if applicable).

How can I track my application?

Use the Track Application feature with your application number or mobile.

Payment failed but money deducted?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Contact us if delayed.

Having urgent technical issues?

For critical issues affecting your application deadline, call us directly at 0177-2625924 during business hours.
